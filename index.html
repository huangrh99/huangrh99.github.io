Runhui Huang (黄润辉)

huangrh9@gmail.com

Hi, I am currently a PhD student at the University of Hong Kong, under the supervision of Prof. Hengshuang Zhao. Previously, I received my Bachelor's degree (2021) and Master's degree (2024) from Sun Yat-sen University, where I was supervised by Prof. Xiaodan Liang.

My research focuses on Unified Multimodal Understanding and Generation, with broad interests in vision-language pre-training and post-training.
